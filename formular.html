About

Digital forms offer a variety of options and often enable faster and more effective use compared to analog forms. For this task, we chose a form and digitized it intending to save the user time and effort. I picked the Application for Granting Travel expenses reimbursement, since I considered the form to require improvement. In my opinion, it is quite overloaded, not very well structured and therefore. To change this, I restructured the form and divided it into sections. Unnecessary fields or fields that can be filled in using the computer (calculations) have been removed or are being filled in in advance. The integrated functions such as drop-down menus, date pickers and the direct upload of files without having to have paper copies, save a lot of time and ensure a good overview.
