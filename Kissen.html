About

Typography is not only used on paper or screens. Typography can also be perceptible, it can be experienced. This work was about creating typography inspired by one we found in our near surrounding. After designing the font, we had to present it in a three-dimensional way, it should be present in space. The word we used had to represent what we connect with the Font.
I found the lettering you see below in our University and thought it was quite interesting. I liked the playful and unique look of it, so I choose it to be the base of my font.

In the second step I started sketching my letters on paper. I tried to take over the main characteristics of the lettering, but also to give them a new and personal touch.

I then digitalized the resulting drafts, using Adobe Illustrator.

For the final realization in the room, I decided to use a pillow on which the lettering was embroidered. I sewed the pillow myself, the lettering was applied with the help of a textile designer using a machine.
